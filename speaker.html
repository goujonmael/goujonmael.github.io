My Bluetooth Speaker



My 2 x 30 Watts Bluetooth Speaker
Inspired by my previous Bluetooth speakers built, here is my latest creation.

As you can see it looks quite futuristic, because I painted it in white and mat gray. Two sides are made of TPU (flexible material to reduce vibrations with the ground) printed in 3D.

Speakers

It has two full range speakers of 30 Watts 4 Ohms each (ND91-4 from Dayton Audio). I added two passive radiator to make it waterproof and improve the bass rendering

Easy to use

On the top there are 5 buttons to pause / play, go to the next / previous music and raise / lower the volume.


TDA7492

It is based on a TDA7492 (2 x 50W amplifier) which can modify its gain so as not to burn the speakers.


Multifonction

The speaker can be powered by 12v or with a battery pack to be portable.
